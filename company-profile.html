Welcome to my website!

I'm Peter Peabody, an experienced and dedicated driving instructor based in Basingstoke and serving the surrounding areas. With a passion for helping people gain their independence and confidence on the road, I am committed to providing top-quality driving instruction tailored to each student’s needs. My Journey Having spent several years working for a large driving school, I gained invaluable experience teaching learners of all backgrounds and skill levels. I have helped countless students pass their driving tests and become safe, responsible drivers. Now, I am excited to be working independently, bringing my years of expertise and personalized approach directly to you. Going freelance means I can offer more flexibility in scheduling, a more personalized teaching approach, and competitive pricing. My Approach Every driver is unique, so I adapt my teaching style to suit your learning pace and preferences. Whether you’re a first-time learner, need a confidence boost, or require refresher lessons, I’ll be there to guide you every step of the way. My patient and friendly manner creates a relaxed and supportive environment, making the learning experience as enjoyable and stress-free as possible. Why Choose Me? Experienced & Qualified: I am a fully qualified driving instructor with years of experience in helping students pass their driving tests and become confident drivers. Local Knowledge: As someone who has worked extensively in Basingstoke and the surrounding areas, I can prepare you for local roads and routes, giving you an edge when it comes to your driving test.
